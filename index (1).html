Meet the founder

AJ

Founder

Ammbica Jain

IT Professional  ·  Transformational Coach  ·  Lifelong Learner

For many years, I built my career in IT before taking a break to dedicate myself to my family. After moving to the United States, I embraced that chapter with gratitude — but deep inside, I felt something was missing. Not from my life, but from my voice.

As I met women from different backgrounds, I realised I was not alone. So many of us quietly carry dreams, questions, fears, and strengths that never find the space to be expressed. We spend years supporting others while slowly forgetting ourselves.

That realisation became the beginning of SunRi-Ssakhi. Because when a woman finds her voice, she does not just change her own life — she inspires others to find theirs.
